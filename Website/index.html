Why RKS?

Education

RKS was founded with a focus on fostering and educating the local kink community. We host workshops and classes typically twice a month from presenters ranging from our own members, to regional talent, and even nationally known educators.

We are Open

We are a member-run BDSM organization dedicated to openness, tolerance, safety, and education. We do not discriminate on the basis of gender, sexual orientation, or fetish.

Members and guests are expected to attend RKS functions with an open mind and mutual respect, as well as learn and practice scene etiquette.

We have Space

Our huge dungeon located in Rochester, NY offers fantastic options right in your own Western New York backyard. Whether you live 5 minutes or 50 miles away, the RKS dungeon is one of a kind in this area. With multiple stations to play, there is a place for everyone to feel comfortable and explore in the ways that they love best.

Thanks to a vibrant community of dedicated members and volunteers, RKS has become a 2nd home to many and a family to its membership

2. Talk to as many RKS Members and Board Members as possible

3. Email the RKS Board to attend a party/workshop/event

Attend a few parties. Every person seeking membership needs to have a sponsor, a current member in good standing who is willing to vouch for you. After attending a party, you can join in on our forums to build friendships and find sponsors.

5. Email the Board again and let them know you want to join

They will get back to you as soon as possible. Again, the more members you talk to before asking to join, the faster the response.
