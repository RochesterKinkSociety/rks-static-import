Full Steam Ahead - RKS' 20th Anniversary Weekend!

Rochester Kink Society invites you to spend the weekend with us as we celebrate our 20th Anniversary! We have a weekend full of events you don't want to miss out on! Celebrate two decades of decadence with us as we move Full Steam Ahead into the future of RKS!

October 7-9, 2016

On Friday, October 7 join us for a Steam Punk Themed Kinky Carnival!
We'll be open from 7 p.m. to midnight!
Games! Food! Pony Cart Rides! Prizes!
Cost for Friday's Event - $10 for RKS members (all levels) and $15 for approved guests

Then on Saturday, October 8 we have our 20th Annual Kink Auction!
Doors will open at 1:30 p.m. and the auction will start promptly at 2 p.m.
Live Auction! Silent Auction! Over 100 items up for grabs from vendors all over the country!
Buy It Now Area! Door Prizes and Raffles!!!
Take a look at the Auction Site and check back often - new items will be added regularly!

THEN - Join us on Saturday evening for our Anniversary Masquerade Play Party!
We'll be partying from 8 p.m. until 2 a.m.!
Participation in the theme is encouraged, but never required!

Pricing for Saturday's Events:
Auction - FREE for RKS Members, $5 for approved guests
Masquerade Play Party - free to silver/gold members, $15 for bronze members AND for any guest who attended the Auction, $25 guest fee

Finally, on Sunday, October 9 join us for Aftercare Sunday Brunch!
RKS will provide coffee and pancakes, please bring a dish to pass!
Brunch, cuddling, and socializing!
We'll be open from 10 a.m. until 2 p.m.

FREE for all members and guests with a dish to pass or other donation to RKS.

All events are open to RKS members, members of reciprocal groups, and approved guests!
Send questions and/or RSVPs to rks@rochesterkinksociety
